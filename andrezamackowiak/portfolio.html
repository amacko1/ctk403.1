Videos

Big Citizens

I edited this film in collaboration with a team on Television that I worked for. This series, shares about how people who have dwarfism live their life. It is about accessibility, preconception, and bullying. This series has three episodes, each video tells about the history of different characters (different perspectives).

Promoting Childhood

I teamed up with the NGO World Vision to edit this moving and strong video. It tells the story of some children, from a poor town in the backlands of Brazil, who lose their childhood to work because they need to help their parents.

Brazilia Teimosa

I edited and produced this film with a team for the Television that I worked for. It was a special edition about a strategic, privileged, and coveted space in the capital of Pernambuco, Brazilia Teimosa. It is located at the beginning of the South Zone of Recife. It shares about the warrior community, made up of simple, happy and receptive people, who mostly make their living from fishing and commercial activities, today the population faces constant concern; of losing their homes to the large construction companies that aim to build the business and residential buildings due to the extraordinary vision that Brasília Teimosa has and the millionaire projects that surround it.

Mayor Neco

I teamed up with a video producer in Brazil to edit some videos for the political campaign for Mayor Neco. In this video I edited this project, which was close to the format of a mini-documentary, it is about a space that the mayor had created in his first term, COMPAZ. Compaz, is a building that has computers with internet access, study rooms, and a children's area with two art educators. Among the activities are, storytelling; theatrical, musical, circus, and literary performances; dialogue circles with themes of combating drugs, racism, homophobia, violence against women.
