Andreza Mackowiak

I am Andreza Mackowiak. I'm from Brazil, but I'm a permanent resident in the United States, based in Normal, Illinois.
I have a Bachelor's Degree in Social Communication with an emphasis in Advertising and Marketing from de University Mauricio de Nassau in Brazil. I am currently a graduate student at Illinois State University, pursuing a Master's degree in Creative Technologies, focusing on Web and Graphic Design. Additionally, I am employed in the Technology Solutions Department as a Web Designer - Graduate Assistant.

I consider myself a Graphic designer and videomaker. My style is characterized by strong color, and vintage and sophisticated design. Each project aims to create work that is both beautiful and useful.
I worked seven years on television in Brasil - 3 years as an intern - producing and editing journalistic videos and mini-documentaries. I have experiences as a Graphic design in some companies, and for few years (+ thanks to the power of the internet) I leveraged a few projects into a thriving freelance business! For 1 year I am adventuring in the web design area, actually in codes - HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I know from experience that building a creative career can be a challenging and overwhelming endeavor. When I'm not working on client projects or in the office, you can find me taking pictures in the state that I adopted, Illinois (Click to see), or drawing some illustrations just for fun, but it sometimes works in some project of Graphic design.
